Our Mission



We believe in making fashion affordable to everyone around the world and that is our global mission. In order to achieve this goal for every piece of clothing you shop from us we provide a kid in need with the same piece of clothing you purchased.

For example, in Kenya, one of our goals is to release people from fetching water so they have free time to engage in economic or personal development activities.

The Hourglass works with governments and charities around the world to make this mission a reality. We currently have head quarters in India and Kenya to deliver our products to those in need.

What?



For every item you purchase from us we will donate a piece of clothing of equal value to someone in need.

Why?



It is belief and mission that everyone should have equal access to clothing and fashion to express themselves the way they want. We also partner with local charities to provide help and relief to those in need. Your support enables us to responsd to crises immediately and protect the affected communities.

How?



We partner with big charities in India and Kenya to provide essential goods to those in need. We also fund immediatly when disasters strike.

Where?



Currently our foundation has partnered with charities in Canada, India and Kenya.
