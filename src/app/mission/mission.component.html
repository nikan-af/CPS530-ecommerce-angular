Our Mission



The outcome we seek is that a young person is able to move from one life stage to the next – whether that’s from unemployment to training, from training to an apprenticeship, from homelessness to a family.

For example, in Kenya, one of our goals is to release people from fetching water so they have free time to engage in economic or personal development activities.

The Hourglass Foundation focuses on working with our long-term trusted partners to achieve sustainable change, but also engages directly with young people at a grass-roots level.

Hourglass is the primary funder of the Hourglass Foundation and supported by our employees and customers. In addition, Hourglass makes donations providing space, equipment, expertise, management resource and time to run hands-on training schemes and projects with Hourglass volunteers.

What?



The Hourglass Foundation creates opportunities by providing inspiration, support, infrastructure and training. By helping disadvantaged young people overcome barriers and change their lives for the better, we help them achieve their full potential.

Why?



We believe in empowering our customers to be confident and express themselves – but we recognise the inequalities present in conditions and opportunities for young people globally. By supporting and investing in support for those people, we are working for a brighter future we can all be a part of.

How?



We fund projects in collaboration with long-term trusted partners, and we also engage directly with young people at a grass-roots level.

Where?



Our work is focused on long-term and carefully selected projects in the UK, India and Kenya.
