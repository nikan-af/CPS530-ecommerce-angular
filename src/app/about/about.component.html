Who we are

Hourglass Clothing store is an online fashion retailer. We are headquarterd in downtonw Toronto, Canada and our designs are inspired by our city. We are a passionate team of designers and fashion enthusiasts who believe in the freedom of expression, we believe you are your own boss and you can express yourself in the clothing you choose for yourself.

Our Product

Our products are all manufactured in Toronto, Canada. Detail matters to our designers. Our Technical Designers pay close attention to every stitch in our products to ensure that the product meets our high standards. To source our fabrics we contact the manufacturers directly to avoid the middlemen and reduce prices for our customers.

Free Shipping

We are all about making fashion affordable for everyone and therefore we provide free shipping in Canada from coast to coast. We also provide free next-day shipping in the US if you sign up for the premier delivery service.
